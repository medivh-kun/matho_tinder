As a subject, mathematics is surprisingly hard to pin down with one catch-all definition.

There is no such thing as “the most interesting aspect of maths”. Yes, numbers are cool, but so are geometry, topology, computer science, cryptography. Mathematics is not simply stuff to do with numbers, but a huge range of topics. So, the idea behind this game is that one can always explore the maths that they most enjoy. The game is simple enough: you swipe or heart to choose between 2 cards, and the card not chosen will be replaced. Each card represents a mathematical topic with a brief intro on the first page, and a side story or application on the second page. It’s the mathmo version of Who’d you rather.
Still not sure how the game works? Play a demo game here with some animal pictures

Disclaimer: The content of the cards are subject to my own research and preferences, and therefore not representative of the complexities of the Mathematics field. However, I hope it can be a good start to learning something new for you!
